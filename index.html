What We Do

Our Services

Specialist flooring, painting and decorating across London and the UK - quality you can see, results that last.

Hardwood & Laminate Flooring

Professional supply and installation of solid hardwood, engineered wood and laminate flooring. We ensure perfectly level subfloors, precise cutting around skirting boards, and a flawless finish in every room.

LVT & Vinyl Flooring

Expert fitting of luxury vinyl tile (LVT), click vinyl and sheet vinyl flooring - ideal for kitchens, bathrooms and high-traffic areas. Waterproof, durable, and available in a wide range of wood and stone effects.

Floor Tiling

Precision floor tiling for kitchens, bathrooms, hallways and living spaces. We work with porcelain, ceramic, natural stone and large-format tiles - laid straight, diagonal or in herringbone patterns to your specification.

Interior Painting & Decorating

Full interior painting and decorating service including walls, ceilings, woodwork and trim. From smooth single-colour finishes to bold feature walls - we prepare every surface properly for a result that lasts for years.

Wallpapering

Professional wallpaper hanging for all wallpaper types - paste-the-wall, paste-the-paper, textured, patterned and luxury materials. Perfect pattern matching, seamless joins and a bubble-free finish guaranteed.

Exterior Painting

Weather-resistant exterior painting for houses, flats and commercial properties. We use premium masonry and weatherproof paints to protect and refresh facades, render, soffits, fascias and garden walls - built to withstand the British climate.
